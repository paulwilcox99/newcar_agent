How to run auto dealership simulation with your agent:

1) In step mode:
	in one terminal:
		cd ./auto-dealership
		.venv/bin/python main.py --reset --days 104 --seed 42 --disable-workers new_cars --step
	in second terminal
		cd ./newcar_agent
		.venv/bin/python agent.py

2) In live mode (year every 10 seconds)
	in one terminal:
		cd ./auto-dealership
		.venv/bin/python main.py --reset --days 104 --seed 42 --disable-workers new_cars --delay 10
	in second terminal
		cd ./newcar_agent
		.venv/bin/python agent.py
